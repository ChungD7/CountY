Our Data

We source our data from the University of Michigan's ICPSR (Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research) database. The data has been shared with the intention to be used for instructional purposes, and remains such for this project.

The dataset we chose details the 2016 presidential election and was led by the American Political Science Association. The data files are packaged by ICPSR in TSV and only the file-types were modified by us so that we're able to serve the results to you on this site.

Limitations to this data may include the randomness of the sample set of some survey results included in our site. The scope of our offerings is also limited to only the 2016 US presidential election. Our team offers purely recirculated data, and does not claim responsibility for any unwanted effects from interpreting our statistics.

For the best possible experience with our software, we advise all users to periodically run CTRL+Shift+R to refresh any changes we make to our static files, including our stylesheets and map logic.
